Outdork


An android application that encourages people to be more outdoors, it gives examples of tracks and other outdoor activities to do. Outdork was a school project where I was a part in a team of 4 people. It was written in Kotlin using Android Studio and Firebase for storing. The application is fully functioning, press the image to find it on GitHub.

Zetkin


Zetkin foundation is an organization that provides a tool for organizational activism and helps organizations with organizing memberships, events and activities. I have been volunteering to help with the frontend development of the platform for about 2 years on my free time. It is a great organization and I have learned a great deal from them. Primarily JavaScript and its friends such as Typescript, Cypress, React, CSS and much more revolving the world of JavaScript.
